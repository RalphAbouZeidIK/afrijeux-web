Self-Exclusion Program

Take control with our self-exclusion options designed to help you manage your gaming habits.

How It Works

Self-exclusion allows you to voluntarily restrict your access to lottery services for a set period of time. During this period, you will not be able to purchase tickets, access your account, or participate in any lottery games.

You can choose exclusion periods ranging from 30 days to permanent exclusion.

Request Self-Exclusion

To enroll in our self-exclusion program, please contact our support team or visit your account settings. You'll need to verify your identity and select your desired exclusion period.
